Driver License Services Alert: Attention Texans! On May 31, 2025, at 7pm CT, the legacy driver license renewal application will no longer be available. If you’re eligible for online driver license/ID services, please use Texas by Texas (TxT) to complete your transaction. Review eligibility criteria here. Visit Texas.gov/TxT to learn more and create your account.

Ready to take the wheel?

Step into the driver’s seat with TxT. Now you can upgrade your learner license to a provisional license online with TxT. Create your account, link your current Texas driver license, and check your TxT to-do list for the upgrade option after you’ve passed your driving skills test. You’ll be road ready in no time.
