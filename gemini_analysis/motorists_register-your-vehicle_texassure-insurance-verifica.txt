## Product Requirements Document: TexasSure - Insurance Verification Page

**1. Introduction**

This document outlines the content requirements for recreating the TexasSure - Insurance Verification page on the TxDMV.gov website, found at [http://www.txdmv.gov/motorists/register-your-vehicle/texassure-insurance-verification](http://www.txdmv.gov/motorists/register-your-vehicle/texassure-insurance-verification). The goal is to provide the necessary information for an AI implementor to accurately reproduce the page's content.

**2. Main Content Area Requirements**

The main content area should include the following content elements and structure.

**2.1. Breadcrumbs**

The breadcrumb navigation should display the following links, in order:

*   Home (Link to "/")
*   Motorists (Link to "/motorists")
*   Register Your Vehicle (Link to "/motorists/register-your-vehicle")

**2.2. Page Title**

The page title should be:

*   "TexasSure - Insurance Verification"

**2.3. Content Block: TexasSure Information**

This section contains information about the TexasSure program. It should include the following:

*   **Image:** A logo associated with TexasSure, with the file name “texassure\_logo.png”, with dimensions approximately 150px wide and 39px high. It should be located on the right side of the text. Source for the image: "/images/texassure_logo.png".

*   **Paragraph 1:** "TexasSure is designed to reduce the number of uninsured drivers and cut costs for responsible Texans, who now pay almost $900 million a year to protect themselves against those with no coverage. Currently, an estimated 20 percent of Texas drivers are uninsured."

*   **Paragraph 2:** "The automated database is a joint project of Texas' departments of motor vehicles, insurance, public safety and information resources, and quickly identifies vehicle owners without mandatory insurance coverage."

*   **Paragraph 3:** "More information about insurance verification is available on the [TexasSure website](http://www.texassure.com/)." This should be a hyperlink that opens in a new tab.

**2.4. How can we make this page better for you?**

This section collects feedback. The feedback information is not included.
