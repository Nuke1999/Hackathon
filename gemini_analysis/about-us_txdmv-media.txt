## Product Requirements Document: TxDMV.gov Media Page

**1. Introduction**

This document outlines the content requirements for recreating the "Media" page of the TxDMV.gov website. It focuses on the main content area and essential links, excluding general elements like navigation menus and footers.

**2. Goal**

To accurately reproduce the content of the TxDMV Media page, enabling users to access press releases, social media links, and contact information.

**3. Detailed Content Requirements**

**3.1. Introductory Text:**

*   **Text:** "The Texas Department of Motor Vehicles welcomes questions from the news media."
*   **Text:** "Please send media inquiries via email to <a href="mailto:Media@TxDMV.gov">Media@TxDMV.gov</a> or call our media contact, Adam Shaivitz, at (512) 465-1484."
*   **Text:** "If you are not with the news media please call 1-888-368-4689 or visit our main contact page via our website's header."
*   **Text:** "TxDMV budget and expenditure numbers are available at <a href="http://www.texastransparency.org" target="_blank">www.texastransparency.org</a>."

**3.2. Driver's Privacy Protection Act Call to Action:**

*   **Link Text:** "Driver's Privacy Protection Act and Public Information"
*   **Link URL:** "https://prod-origin.txdmv.gov/drivers-privacy-protection-act"

But please be aware that for some inquiries we may ask you to follow up with us by <a href="/contact-us#phone">phone or email</a>, especially if we’ll need your personal information (vehicle identification number, address, etc.) in order to research your question."
