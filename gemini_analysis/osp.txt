## Product Requirements Document: TxDMV Online Services Page

**1. Introduction**

This document details the content requirements for recreating the TxDMV Online Services page (http://www.txdmv.gov/osp). It focuses exclusively on the textual content and the relationships to other pages within the site or external resources. Design elements and general site features like navigation and search are excluded.

**2. Goal**

To provide clear, concise specifications for content to allow an AI implementor to accurately recreate the information presented on the TxDMV Online Services page.

**3. Scope**

This document covers the following content areas:

*   Main content area including the cards for:
    *   Vehicle Registration Renewal
    *   Renewal Recipient Address Change
    *   Vehicle Transfer Notification
    *   Regional Service Center
    *   Specialty License Plates
    *   Go Paperless
*   Feedback form
*   Footer text and navigation
*   Header Navigation

**4. Detailed Content Specifications**

**4.1. Main Content Area:**

The main content area consists of a series of cards, each representing a different online service.

**4.1.1. Vehicle Registration Renewal Card:**

*   **Title:** "Vehicle Registration Renewal"
*   **Content:**
    *   "TxDMV offers two options to renew your vehicle registration online."
    *   "Renew your vehicle registration using the Texas by Texas (TxT) website or mobile application" - This is a link to "/osp-texas-by-texas".
    *   "Renew your vehicle registration using TxDMV website" - This is a link to "/osp-registration-renewal"
    *   "Learn more about vehicle registration renewal →" - This is a link to "/motorists/register-your-vehicle".

**4.1.2. Renewal Recipient Address Change Card:**

*   **Title:** "Renewal Recipient Address Change"
*   **Content:**
    *   "Be sure your registration renewal notice follows you wherever you go."
    *   "Change the address at which your vehicle registration renewal notice is received." - This is a link to "/osp-address-change".

**4.1.3. Vehicle Transfer Notification Card:**

*   **Title:** "Vehicle Transfer Notification"
*   **Content:**
    *   "Selling your vehicle? A vehicle transfer notification updates the motor vehicle record to show the vehicle has sold."
    *   "Submit a Vehicle Transfer Notification online" - This is a link to "/osp-vehicle-transfer-notification".
    *   "Learn more about submitting a Vehicle Transfer Notification →" - This is a link to "/osp-vehicle-transfer-learn-more".

**4.1.4. Regional Service Center Card:**

*   **Title:** "Regional Service Center"
*   **Content:**
    *   "TxDMV Regional Service Centers offer same-day and next-day appointments."
    *   "Schedule an appointment with a TxDMV Regional Service Center (RSC)" - This is a link to "/osp-schedule-appointment".
    *   "Learn more about services provided at TxDMV RSCs →" - This is a link to "https://www.txdmv.gov/regional-service-centers".

**4.1.5. Specialty License Plates Card:**

*   **Title:** "Specialty License Plates"
*   **Content:**
    *   "MyPlates provides customized and/or specialty license plates for Texas motorists."
    *   "Order a specialty license plate at MyPlates.com" - This is a link to "/osp-myplates".
    *   "Learn more about military and disabled veteran license plates →" - This is a link to "https://www.txdmv.gov/motorists/for-our-troops".

**4.1.6. Go Paperless Card:**

*   **Title:** "Go Paperless"
*   **Content:**
    *   "Registration renewal notices are available via email instead of postal mail."
    *   "Switch to electronic renewal notices" - This is a link to "/osp-go-paperless".
