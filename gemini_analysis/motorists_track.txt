## Product Requirements Document: TxDMV "Where's My Sticker?" Page

**1. Introduction**

This document outlines the requirements for recreating the "Where's My Sticker?" page on the TxDMV website. The goal is to provide a clear and concise set of specifications to allow for accurate recreation of the page's content. This document focuses solely on content and functionality, excluding design elements.

**2. Goal**

Enable users to track the status of their vehicle registration sticker.

**3. Target Audience**

Texas motorists who have recently renewed their vehicle registration and are awaiting their new sticker.

**4. Page Title**

*   "Where's My Sticker?"

**5. Breadcrumb Navigation**

*   Home > Motorists

**6. Main Content**

*   **Removable Box:**
    *   Text: "**Please allow <u>approximately three weeks</u> for the processing, printing, and mailing of your sticker.**

    **During this time, you may use your registration renewal receipt as proof of current registration for up to 31 days. This receipt is sent to the email address provided at the time of registration.**"
*   **Spacer:** (Creates a 15px space)
*   **Iframe:**
    *   Source: "//apps.txdmv.gov/tools/sticker-tracking/#!/search"
    *   Title: "registration sticker tracking tool"

**7. Bottom Call to Action**

*   **How can we make this page better for you?** Clicking the button will open feedback webform.

**8. Feedback Form**

*   Form Title: **We Value Your Feedback**
*   Form Description: Your message will go to our web team. Thank you for helping us to serve you better!
    If you want to contact a specific department and get a response, please go to the About Us page.
*   Fields:
    *   **Email:** (Required, Email input, Placeholder: "Email")
    *   **Message:** (Required, Textarea, Placeholder: "Your message")
    *   **Send message**
*   Form Submission: Submission of the form should be sent to the TxDMV web team.
