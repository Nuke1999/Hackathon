## Product Requirements Document: TxDMV eLICENSING Resources Page

**1. Introduction**

This document outlines the content requirements for recreating the "eLICENSING Resources" page on the TxDMV.gov website. It focuses on replicating the text and structural content of the page, including links to external resources and downloadable files. Design and functionality elements outside of core content are excluded.

**2. Goal**

To accurately reproduce the content of the eLICENSING Resources page (http://www.txdmv.gov/dealers/licensing/elicensing-resources) ensuring dealers and other licensees have access to critical information regarding the eLICENSING system.

**3. Scope**

This document covers the following content elements:

*   Introductory text explaining eLICENSING
*   Link to eLICENSING login page
*   Information regarding updating contact information
*   Listing of User Guides & Manuals
*   Listing of eLICENSING Forms
*   Listing of Video Resources (both Single-Topic and In-Depth)

**4. Detailed Content Requirements**

The main content area should contain the following elements in the specified order:

**4.2. Introductory Text**

*   **Content Type:** Paragraphs of Text
*   **Text:**
    *   "Motor vehicle dealers, salvage dealers, and industry licensees operating in Texas may now apply for, renew, or amend motor vehicle licenses online. The Texas Department of Motor Vehicles (TxDMV) replaced its paper-based licensing system with eLICENSING, an online self-service hub for all motor vehicle licensees and salvage dealers operating in Texas. The new system became available in March 2017."
    *   "eLICENSING provides many user benefits, including 24-hour access to licensing information, the elimination of paper processing, the ability to submit license applications and payments online, a guided application process facilitating faster approvals, and the ability to track the progress of submitted applications."
    *   "To receive email updates regarding eLICENSING, please visit [www.TxDMV.gov/subscribe](https://public.govdelivery.com/accounts/TXDMV/subscriber/new) and check the box beside the Dealers and Manufacturers subscription topic."

**4.3. Contact Information Update Section**

*   **Content Type:** Heading and Paragraphs of Text
